1. Prompt Engineering Kya Hai?

"Prompt ka matlab hai wo hidayat ya sawal jo aap AI ko dete hain. Prompt Engineering ka matlab hai un hidayat ko itna wazeh aur technical banana ke AI ka jawab bilkul waisa hi ho jaisa aap chahte hain."

3. Ek Acha Prompt Kaise Likhein? (The Framework)

Ek professional prompt likhne ke liye aapko **C-R-E-S** framework follow karna chahiye:
1. Context: AI ko batayein ke wo kaun hai (e.g., You are a Doctor).
2. Role: Use batayein ke usne karna kya hai (e.g., Write a prescription).
3. Exclusion: Use batayein ke kya nahi karna (e.g., Do not use complex medical terms).
4. Specificity: Use batayein ke format kaisa ho (e.g., Give output in a table). Is framework ko seekh kar aap kisi bhi AI se apna gulam banwa sakte hain.
