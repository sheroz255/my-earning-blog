## 4. Operational Excellence: From Zero to $10k/Month

The secret to scaling is "Modularization." You shouldn't build every project from scratch. Create **"Snapshots"** or templates that can be deployed to multiple clients in the same niche with minimal changes.

### The Retainer Model
Never sell a "One-Time" setup only. Charge a $2,000 setup fee and a **$500/month Maintenance Fee**. This ensures your income is passive and predictable. As your AI systems handle more data, they become more valuable to the client, making it impossible for them to fire you.
