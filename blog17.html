1. CPA Marketing Kya Hai aur Ye Kaam Kaise Karti Hai?

CPA ka matlab hai "Cost Per Action". Bari companies jaise Amazon, Netflix, aur gaming apps apne promotion ke liye CPA networks ka sahara leti hain. Jab aap unke links ko share karte hain aur koi user us link par click karke apna email enter karta hai, toh aapko foran commission mil jata hai. Ye Affiliate Marketing se asaan hai kyunke yahan user ki pocket se paise kharch nahi hote, sirf information share hoti hai.

2. Top CPA Networks 2025

Shuruat karne ke liye aapko sahi network ka intikhab karna hota hai. Kuch mashhoor networks ye hain:

  • CPAlead: Mobile app installs ke liye best hai.

3. Traffic Kaise Hasil Karein? (The Secret Sauce)

CPA mein sabse bari cheez traffic hai. Aap 2 tarah se traffic la sakte hain:
Free Traffic: Facebook Groups, Pinterest, aur Quora ka istemal karein. Log giveaway offers (jaise "Win iPhone 15") par bohot jaldi click karte hain.
Paid Traffic: Google Ads, Facebook Ads, ya Pop-under ads (jaise Adsterra) ka istemal kar ke aap thore se paise laga kar hazaron dollars kama sakte hain. Hum recommend karte hain ke shuruat free traffic se karein.

4. Content Locking Strategy

Content locking aik aisi technique hai jahan aap kisi qimti file (jaise PDF book ya software) ko lock kar dete hain. User ko wo file download karne ke liye aik chota sa survey ya task pura karna hota hai. Jaise hi user task pura karta hai, aapko paise mil jate hain aur user ko file. Ye 2025 mein passive income ka sabse bara zariya hai.
