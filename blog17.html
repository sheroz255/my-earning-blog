4. AI-Driven Customer Retention

Acquiring a new customer is 5 times more expensive than keeping an old one. In 2026, advanced stores use AI agents to handle personalized follow-ups, birthday discounts, and predictive upsells. If a customer buys a high-end blender, the AI automatically sends them a recipe book three days later and offers a discount on a replacement blade six months later. This "LTV" (Lifetime Value) focus is the secret to sustainable wealth.

5. Mastering Global Logistics and Taxes

To be a global player, you must understand the "boring" side of the business: VAT, Customs, and Import Duties. In 2026, automation tools now handle these calculations at checkout, so there are no surprises for the customer. Understanding "Incoterms" and freight forwarding allows you to move bulk inventory via sea or air cargo, significantly lowering your COGS (Cost of Goods Sold) and exploding your profit margins.
