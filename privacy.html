Privacy & Data Protection Policy

1. Introduction to Our Commitment

EarnSmart Portal ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your personal data and privacy. This comprehensive document details how we handle information across all our sub-domains and services. By using our platform, you agree to the terms outlined in this 5000+ words policy framework designed to comply with International Data Protection Regulations (GDPR) and local laws.

2. Information We Collect (In-Depth)

Our data collection is divided into two categories: Personal Data and Usage Data. Personal Data: This includes email addresses provided during newsletter signups or contact forms. Usage Data: We automatically collect information such as IP addresses, browser versions, the pages you visit on our site, the time and date of your visit, and unique device identifiers. This helps us understand which earning guides are most effective for our community.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. A cookie is a small file placed on your device. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies, but some parts of our site may not function correctly. Our advertising partners, like Adsterra, also use cookies to serve ads based on your interests. This is essential for keeping our high-quality content free for everyone.

4. Third-Party Links & Safety

Our blogs contain "Smart Links" and external resources. While we verify every link at the time of publication, we do not have control over the content of third-party sites. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit. We are not responsible for any data loss or misuse on external platforms.

5. Data Security Protocols

Your data's security is our top priority. We implement SSL encryption and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.
