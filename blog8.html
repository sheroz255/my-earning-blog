Amazon Affiliate Marketing Kya Hai?

Affiliate marketing ka matlab hai Amazon ki products ko promote karna. Jab koi aapke diye gaye link se kuch kharidta hai, toh Amazon aapko uska commission deta hai. Ye commission 1% se lekar 10% tak ho sakta hai, jo ke dollar mein milta hai.

Kamyabi Ke Liye Sahi Niche Ka Intikhab

Amazon par lakho products hain, lekin aap sab kuch promote nahi kar sakte. Aapko aik khas topic (Niche) chun-na hoga, jaise:

  • Tech & Gadgets: Laptops, Mobiles aur Accessories.
  • Home & Kitchen: Coffee makers, Air fryers wagera.
  • Health & Fitness: Yoga mats, Supplements aur Gym equipment.

Passive Income Ka Zariya

Amazon Affiliate ka sabse bara faida ye hai ke ye aik passive income hai. Aik baar jab aapka blog ya social media page rank ho jata hai, toh aap sote waqt bhi commission kamate rehte hain. Is mein shuru mein mehnat lagti hai lekin baad mein phal milta hai.

Amazon Account Kaise Banayein?

Aapko 'Amazon Associates' ki website par ja kar sign up karna hoga. Pakistan se bhi aap apni details add karke account bana sakte hain. Payment ke liye aap Payoneer ka istemal kar sakte hain jo ke Pakistan mein asani se attach ho jata hai.
