1. App Development Kaise Shuru Karein?

App development shuru karne ke liye aapko languages seekhni parti hain. 2025 mein **Kotlin** aur **Flutter** sabse behtareen options hain. Kotlin official Android language hai, jabke Flutter se aap aik hi code se Android aur iPhone (iOS) dono ke liye apps bana sakte hain. Shuruat mein aapko programming ke basics jaise variables, loops, aur functions seekhne honge.

2. Tool of Choice: Android Studio

Android Studio dunya ka sabse powerful software hai app banane ke liye. Is mein aap apni app ka design (UI) taiyar karte hain aur piche coding (Logic) likhte hain. Shuru mein ye mushkil lag sakta hai, lekin jaise hi aap pehli "Hello World" app banayenge, aapka confidence barh jayega...

3. Paisay Kaise Kamayein? (Monetization Strategy)

App se kamai karne ke kai tareeqe hain:

  • Google AdMob: Apni app mein ads dikhayein aur har click par paise kamayein.
  • In-App Purchases: App ke andar premium features bechein.
  • Paid Apps: Play Store par app ki keemat rakhein.
Agar aapki app 100,000 log download kar lete hain, toh aapki mahana kamai $2000+ tak ja sakti hai.

4. Play Store Par App Publish Karna

Jab app ban jaye, toh usay Google Play Console par upload karna hota hai. Iske liye aapko aik dafa $25 ki fees deni parti hai, aur phir aap dunya bhar mein apni app launch kar sakte hain. SEO (App Store Optimization) lazmi karein taake log aapki app search mein asani se dhondh sakein.
