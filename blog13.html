2. Finding a Profitable App Niche

The biggest mistake in the app business is building something "for everyone." In 2026, the real profit is in "Micro-Niches." Instead of a general fitness app, build an "AI-Powered Yoga Coach for Seniors." By solving a specific problem for a specific group of people, you reduce your marketing costs and increase your app's value. Use App Store Optimization (ASO) tools to find keywords with high search volume but low competition.
