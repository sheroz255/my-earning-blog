1. Facebook Page vs Professional Profile

Pehle sirf Pages monetize hote thay, lekin ab Facebook ne "Professional Mode" launch kar diya hai. Iska matlab hai ke aap apni purani profile se bhi paise kama sakte hain. Page un logon ke liye behtar hai jo aik brand banana chahte hain, jabke Professional Profile unke liye hai jo apni personal life aur videos ko monetize karna chahte hain. Dono suraton mein content original hona lazmi hai.

2. In-Stream Ads: Videos se Dollar Kamayein

Ye Facebook se paise kamanay ka sabse mashhoor tareeqa hai. Jab aap lambi videos (3 minutes se zyada) upload karte hain, toh Facebook unke darmyan ads dikhata hai. In ads se honay wali kamayi ka 55% hissa aapko milta hai. Pakistan mein reh kar aap "Rights Manager" ya "RDP" ka istemal kar ke asani se monetization on karwa sakte hain.

Monetization Criteria 2025:
  • Kam se kam 5,000 Followers (Jo ke bohot asaan hai).
  • Pichle 60 dinon mein 60,000 minutes ka watch time mukammal karna.
  • Kam se kam 5 active videos aapke page par honi chahiyein jo guidelines ke mutabiq hon.

3. Facebook Reels: Short Content ka King

2025 mein Facebook Reels par bohot zyada focus kar raha hai. "Ads on Reels" aik aisa feature hai jahan aapko kisi lambay watch time ki zaroorat nahi hoti. Agar aapki short videos viral hoti hain, toh Facebook aapko invitation bhejta hai. Reels se earning karne ke liye aapko trending music aur high-quality visual content ka istemal karna chahiye.

4. Facebook Marketplace aur E-commerce

Agar aap video nahi bana sakte, toh Marketplace aapke liye hai. Aap local products (jaise kapre, electronics, ya gadgets) ki pictures laga kar unhein apne shehar mein bech sakte hain. Iske liye aapko Facebook ko koi fees nahi deni parti aur saara munafa aapka apna hota hai.

5. Conclusion: Kamyabi Ka Raaz

Facebook earning un logon ke liye hai jo sabr (patience) aur consistency se kaam karte hain. Rozana kam se kam 1 Reel aur hafte mein 3 lambi videos upload karein. Content aisa banayein jo log share karein, kyunke jitni zyada sharing hogi, utni hi zyada aapki earning hogi. Aaj hi apna safar shuru kare
