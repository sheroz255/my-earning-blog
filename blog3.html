YouTube Shorts: 2025 Mein Earning Ka Naya Rasta

YouTube Shorts ne naye creators ke liye earning ke darwaze khol diye hain. Pehle aapko 4000 ghante ka watch time chahiye hota tha, lekin ab aap sirf Shorts views se bhi channel monetize kar sakte hain.

Faceless Channels Ka Trend

Bohat se log apna chehra dikhaye baghair paise kama rahe hain. Aap AI tools ka istemal karke motivational videos, facts, ya funny clips bana sakte hain. Rozana 2-3 shorts upload karne se channel ki growth boht fast hoti hai.

Shorts Se Paise Kamane Ke 3 Tareeqay

1. **YouTube Ad Revenue:** Jab aapka channel monetize ho jata hai, toh YouTube ads ka hissa aapko deta hai. 2. **Sponsorships:** Jab aapke followers barh jate hain, toh brands aapko apni products promote karne ke paise dete hain. 3. **Affiliate Marketing:** Aap kisi product ka link description mein de kar sales par commission kama sakte hain.

Consistency hi kamyabi ki chabi hai. Rozana aik hi waqt par video upload karein aur trending hashtags ka istemal karein.
